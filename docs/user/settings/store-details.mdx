---
title: "Store Details"
description: "Learn how to configure your store's core settings in Spree Commerce."
---

The Store Details section is where you define your store's identity and core operational
preferences — from its name and logo to timezones, units of measurement, and how digital
downloads are handled.

To configure these settings, navigate to **Settings → Store Details** in the admin dashboard.

## Name & Logo

![Name and Logo settings in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/settings/store-details/1-name-and-logo.png)

- **Name** — the public-facing name of your store, displayed in headers, footers, and other
  storefront areas.
- **Logo** — upload a store logo (recommended size: 240x240 px). This image is used only in
  the admin dashboard.

## Currencies & Countries

<Note>Currency, locale, and regional settings are now managed in **Settings → Markets**. See [<u>Markets</u>](https://spreecommerce.org/docs/user/settings/markets) for full details.</Note>

## Standards & Formats

![Standards and Formats settings in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/settings/store-details/2-standards-and-formats.png)

Here you can set the timezone, unit system, and weight units to match your region and business
preferences:

- **Timezone** — set your store's timezone for accurate timestamps and order data.
- **Unit system** — choose between imperial and metric. This affects how weights and measures
  are displayed across your storefront:
  - Imperial system
  - Metric system
- **Weight unit** — choose the default unit for product weights:
  - Pound (lb) — imperial system
  - Ounce (oz) — imperial system
  - Kilogram (kg) — metric system
  - Gram (g) — metric system

## Phone & Address

![Phone and Address settings in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/settings/store-details/3-phone-and-address.png)

Enter your store's contact details here:

- **Phone** — your store's contact phone number.
- **Address** — your business address.

## Digital Assets

![Digital Assets settings in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/settings/store-details/4-digital-assets.png)

Here you can control how digital products (e.g., downloads) are handled after purchase:

- **Limit digital links download days** — set how long download links remain active after
  purchase (in days).
- **Limit digital links download count** — restrict the number of times a download link can
  be used per purchase.

## Saving Changes

After making any updates, click **Update** at the bottom of the page to save your store
settings, otherwise any changes you've made will not be saved.
