---
title: "Built-in Reports"
description: "Learn about the built-in reports available in Spree Commerce."
---

Spree includes ready-made reports that help you quickly analyze sales and product performance without needing external tools.

To view reports, navigate to the **Reports** tab in the admin dashboard.

![Built-in sales reports available in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/reports/reports-tab.png)

By default, two reports are automatically available. Simply click **Show report** to open up the report table view.

## Product Performance Report

The **Product Performance** report shows:

- A list of all products
- Units sold per product
- Revenue generated per product
- Additional product-specific data, including merchandising details and financial metrics

You can:

- Filter by **time period**
- Switch between **currencies** (if multi-currency is supported)
- **Export** the report to CSV

This report is useful for identifying top-performing products, underperforming SKUs, and revenue trends over time.

## Sales Total Report

The **Sales Total** report provides a detailed breakdown of:

- Individual line items
- Associated orders
- Quantities per order
- Financial data (totals, revenue, etc.)

You can:

- Filter by **time period**
- Switch **currency**
- **Export** the report to CSV

This report is especially helpful for financial reconciliation, operational analysis, and deeper order-level review.

If you need reports tailored to your specific business needs or workflows, please refer to [Custom Reports](https://spreecommerce.org/docs/user/reports/custom-reports).
