Updating blog content is a great way to keep your posts accurate, relevant, and SEO-optimized. Whether you’re fixing a typo, updating a link, or revising product details, editing a blog post is simple.

## How to Edit a Blog Post

To edit a blog post, go to **Storefront → Posts** in the admin dashboard.

![The Blog CMS in the Spree Commerce dashboard](/docs/images/user/storefront/posts/edit-post/1-edit-post.png)

Locate the post you want to edit and click on it to open the blog post editor.

![Blog post editor in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/storefront/posts/edit-post/2-post-editor.png)

Make any necessary changes to the content, SEO settings, featured image, or metadata.

When you’re finished, click **Update** to save your changes.

<Warning>Changes won’t be saved unless you click **Update**.</Warning>
