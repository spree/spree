Need to remove outdated or irrelevant content? Deleting a blog post in Spree is quick and easy.

## When to Delete a Post

You might want to delete a post if:

- It's no longer relevant
- It's out of date
- It no longer aligns with your content strategy

## How to Delete a Blog Post

To delete a blog post, navigate to **Storefront â†’ Posts** in the admin dashboard.

![Deleting a blog post in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/storefront/posts/delete-post/1-delete-post.png)

Click on the post you want to delete to open the post editor, scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Delete** in the bottom-right corner.

![Delete a post using the blog CMS in Spree Commerce](/docs/images/user/storefront/posts/delete-post/2-post-editor.png)

Once deleted, the post will be removed from your site immediately.

<Warning>This action cannot be undone. Once deleted, the post and its content cannot be recovered.</Warning>
