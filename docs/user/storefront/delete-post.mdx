---
title: "Delete a Blog Post"
description: "Delete a blog post and manage your entire blog using Spree Commerce."
---

At some point, you might want to delete a post if it’s outdated, no longer relevant, or doesn’t align with your current content strategy.

To delete a post, navigate to the Posts tab of the admin dashboard found under Storefront.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166764/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Post/7.3.4.1_Delete_a_Post_-_Navigate_To_Tab_yzzske.png)

Find the post you’d like to delete and click on it to open the post editor for that specific post.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166764/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Post/7.3.4.2_Delete_a_Post_-_Find_Post_jqc7lg.png)

Scroll to the bottom of the page and locate the red ‘Delete’ button in the bottom right corner under SEO settings.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166764/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Post/7.3.4.3_Delete_a_Post_-_Find_Delete_Button_un8twa.png)

Click ‘Delete’ and the post will be removed from your site.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166764/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Post/7.3.4.4_Delete_a_Post_-_Delete_Post_xcjc0w.png)
