---
title: "Delete a Landing Page"
description: "Delete a landing page you no longer need."
---

When a page is no longer needed, you can remove it to keep your backend organized and clutter-free.

To delete a page, navigate to the Pages tab of the admin dashboard, found under Storefront.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166762/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Page/7.2.3.1_Delete_a_Page_-_Navigate_To_Tab_hkodjz.png)

Find the page you want to delete and click on it. This will open the Theme Editor for that specific page.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166762/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Page/7.2.3.2_Delete_a_Page_-_Click_On_Page_nuwlxn.png)

Click on ‘Page Settings’ found on the right hand side of the top bar Theme Editor. This will open the page settings window.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166762/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Page/7.2.3.3_Delete_a_Page_-_Page_Settings_labnbn.png)

Click the red ‘Delete’ button and the page will be removed from your website.

![](https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1738166762/docs/Storefront/Delete%20a%20Page/7.2.3.4_Delete_a_Page_-_Click_Delete_isenmq.png)

Congratulations, you just de-cluttered your website!
