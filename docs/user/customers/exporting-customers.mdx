Exporting customer data is useful for generating reports, segmenting customer data, or integrating with external tools like email marketing platforms. This makes it easier to analyze customer trends, track engagement, and manage business operations efficiently.

To export customer data, navigate to the Customers tab of the admin dashboard.

## Apply Filters

If you intend to export a segment of the customer base rather than the entire list, you should first apply filters such as location, tags, etc.

## Export

When you’re ready to export the data, simply click ‘Export’ in the top right corner and the results will be emailed to the email address associated with your admin account.

<img alt="Exporting Customers" classname="block dark:hidden" src="https://vendo-production-res.cloudinary.com/image/upload/w_1920/q_auto/v1747661440/docs/Customers/Exporting%20Customers/5.5.3_Exporting_Customers_-_Click_Export_dcwjuy.png" />

Note: You can also access all historical exports in the Audit Log tab of the Settings menu.
