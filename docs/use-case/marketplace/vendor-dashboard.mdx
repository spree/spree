With automated integrations with popular platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce, Spree empowers vendors to effortlessly manage their marketplace offerings, improve sales performance, and deliver exceptional customer service, all within a user-friendly and familiar environment of their own dashboard.

### Vendor Dashboard UX

1. **Products**: Upload and manage inventory, categories, and pricing.
2. **Orders**: Process new orders, update shipping, view status.
3. **Analytics**: Review sales, refunds, and fulfillment metrics.
4. **Settings**: Update storefront profile, payout details, policies.
5. **Support**: Help center, message system, or contact forms.

### Vendor Onboarding Options

- **Shopify**: Connect account to sync products, orders, and shipments.
- **WooCommerce**: Similar plugin-based sync support.
- **Manual or CSV Import**: For vendors not using ecommerce platforms.

### Vendor Profile & Settings

- **Shipping Setup**: Define rates and methods per location.
- **Multi-Language & Currency Support**: Vendors can localize their store presence.
- **Holiday Mode**: Pause shop temporarily.

### Shopify Vendor Operations

- **Product Sync**: Vendors manage inventory in Shopify; marketplace reflects changes.
- **Order Handling**: Orders appear in Shopify admin for normal fulfillment.
- **Fulfillment & Shipment Sync**: Auto-updates customer notifications.
- **Cancellation & Refund Sync**: Fully automated sync of status updates.

### WooCommerce Vendor Operations

- **Product Sync**: Vendors manage inventory in WooCommerce; marketplace reflects changes.
- **Order Handling**: Orders appear in WooCommerce admin for normal fulfillment.
- **Fulfillment & Shipment Sync**: Auto-updates customer notifications.
- **Cancellation & Refund Sync**: Fully automated sync of status updates.

### Manual/CSV Vendor Operations

- **Product Upload**: Add items manually or via CSV import.
- **Order Management**: Review and process orders within the vendor dashboard.
- **Shipment Management**: Add tracking codes, split shipments, mark as fulfilled
- **Cancellations & Refunds**: Access tools to manage returns and exceptions.

### Vendor Financials

- **Automatic or Manual Payouts**: Payout frequency controlled by admin policy.
- **Stripe KYC Flow**: Vendors complete verification during onboarding.
- **Stripe Dashboard Access**: View transactions, disputes, and payout history.
- **Accounting Tools**: Optional integrations with QuickBooks, Xero, etc.
