## Payments

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Stripe" href="/integrations/payments/stripe" img="/images/integrations/logos/stripe.png">
    Stripe is a popular payment gateway that allows you to accept payments from your customers. Credit cards, wallets, buy-now-pay-later, installment payments and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="PayPal" href="/integrations/payments/paypal" img="/images/integrations/logos/paypal.png">
    PayPal is an online payment system that allows you to send and receive money around the world. You can link your credit card, debit card, or bank account. You can also set up a PayPal Balance account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RazorPay" href="/integrations/payments/razorpay" img="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3bcca1bd-5d70-4f0e-9c6d-4f99104d0e93">
    Razorpay is a secure payment gateway that enables businesses to accept online payments via cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets. It also supports international transactions across multiple currencies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
